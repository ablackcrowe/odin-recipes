Oreo Cookie Ball

Description

Bomb Ass Oreo Cookie Balls

Ingredience

  • 1 (6 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 36 Oreo Cookies, finely crushed
  • 16 ouncesd semi-sweet baking chocolate, melted

Steps

  1. Mix cream cheese and cookie crumbs until well blended
  2. Shape into 48 (1-inch) balls. Freeze 10 min. Dip ballsin melted chocolate, place in single layer in shallow waxed paper-lined pan
  3. Refrigerate 1 hour or until firm
