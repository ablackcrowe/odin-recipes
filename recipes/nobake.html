No Bake Cookies

Description

Cookies you DON'T bake... duh

Ingredience

  • 1 1/4 cups white sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 4 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup crunchy peanut butter
  • 3 cups quick-cooking oats
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine sugar, milk,butter, and cocoa. Bring toa boil, and cook for 1 1/2 minutes. Remove from heat, and stir in peanut butter, oats, and vanilla. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto wax paper. Let cool until hardened
